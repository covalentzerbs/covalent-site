the lab.

where we mess around.

AI image and video generation is real, it's fast, and pretending otherwise doesn't make us more principled. The Lab is where we stay fluent in tools we've chosen not to use in client work. Knowing how something works and choosing not to deploy it is a different thing than refusing to look.

what we will and won't do here.

We're not generating synthetic people. We're not building a cheaper version of what we do. The lines exist because we thought about them, not because someone told us to draw them.

Environmental and abstract image research In scope

Texture, atmosphere, form. Used for internal mood development and visual direction work.

Synthetic depictions of real or realistic people Not here

We don't generate human likenesses for any deliverable context.

Pre-viz and moodboard development In scope

If something from The Lab shows up in a pitch, it's there to articulate an idea. The execution is always human.

Replacing production with prompts Not here

The Lab is not a cost-cutting exercise. It doesn't replace crew.
