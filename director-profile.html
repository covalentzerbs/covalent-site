Jacob Frost

cinematic storytelling for modern brands

jacob frost is a los angeles-based director whose work lives at the intersection of raw emotion and refined craft — building worlds that feel lived-in before the first frame is done.

with a background in documentary and commercial production, jacob brings an instinctual eye to beauty, lifestyle, and narrative-driven content. his work across food, fashion, and culture has been recognized internationally and featured across platforms from hbo to adweek.

jacob approaches every project with a deep curiosity for the people and stories behind a brand — translating that into visuals that resonate long after the screen goes dark.
